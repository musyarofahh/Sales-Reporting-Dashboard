# Sales-Reporting-Dashboard
This project focuses on building an interactive dashboard in Power BI to analyze and visualize sales performance over the past year. The dashboard integrates raw sales data into dynamic reports, enabling stakeholders to monitor trends, evaluate performance, and make data‑driven decisions.

#### 🎯 Objectives  
- Provide a **comprehensive view** of sales activities across products, regions, and time periods.  
- Identify **top‑performing products, markets, and sales representatives**.  
- Track **monthly and quarterly sales trends** to highlight growth opportunities and potential risks.  
- Support **strategic planning** with clear, actionable insights.  

#### 🔑 Key Features  
- **Interactive Filters & Drill‑Downs**: Explore sales by product category, region, or customer segment.  
- **Trend Analysis**: Line charts and KPIs to visualize monthly and quarterly performance.  
- **Comparative Insights**: Benchmark current sales against previous periods.  
- **Revenue Breakdown**: Detailed tables and visuals for product and regional contributions.  
- **Forecasting Tools**: Predictive analytics to estimate future sales performance.  

#### 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- **Power BI** for dashboard creation and visualization.  
- **Excel / SQL Databases** as primary data sources.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** for advanced calculations and measures.  

#### 📈 Expected Outcomes  
- Improved **visibility** into sales performance across the organization.  
- Enhanced **decision-making** through real‑time, interactive insights.  
- Identification of **growth opportunities** and underperforming areas.  
- A scalable framework for ongoing **sales monitoring and forecasting**.
